===================================================
   COMMUNICATION TIPS FOR ALZHEIMER CAREGIVERS
===================================================
1. Keep It Simple
   Use short sentences and clear words. Ask one question at a time.
   
2. Establish a Daily Routine
   Keep waking, eating, and sleeping schedules consistent.
   
3. Use Visual and Voice Aids
   Leverage SpiritOS voice assistant, reminder popups, and large-icon interface profiles.
   
4. Keep the Environment Calm
   Minimize loud noises or sudden lighting changes to avoid confusion.
   
5. Patient Reassurance
   If they get lost or confused, speak gently, hold their hand, and explain things calmly.
===================================================
