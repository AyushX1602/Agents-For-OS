===================================================
         SPIRIT OS - QUICKSTART GUIDE
===================================================
Welcome to SpiritOS, the ultimate accessible operating system.

KEY FEATURES & HOW TO ACTIVATE:
1. Hand Gestures:
   - Click the gesture icon on the taskbar to activate camera.
   - Thumbs Up: Open Web Browser
   - Thumbs Down: Close Active Window
   - Pinch: Click/Select

2. Voice Control:
   - Click the microphone icon on the taskbar.
   - Say: "Open Calculator" or "Write note insert shopping list"
   - Say: "Help" or "SOS" in an emergency to alert caregivers.

3. Interactive Profiles:
   - Go to Settings -> Accessibility.
   - Select "Elderly Profile" or "Visually-Impaired Profile" to instantly configure layout, high contrast, and text reader.
